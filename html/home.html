Happiness Care Foundation

Located in New Delhi, India, the Happiness Care Foundation was created in 2002 and has a presence in 25 states. In India, the Happiness Care Foundation for Education worked to support disadvantaged children's education. Their development programme covers education, health, children's and women's livelihoods, and provisions that are equally impacted by resource scarcity.

Help-the-Aged

Indian organisation Help-the-Aged India focuses on senior citizens. Its mission statement is, "Work for the cause and care of under-aged old persons and enhance their living levels." Help-the-Aged works with the federal, state, and local governments as well as advocates. Operates a variety of elderly care programmes. It seeks to provide the elderly who are in need with comprehensive care so they can live honourable lives.

Empower foundation

EmpowerIndia ensures that Adivasis, Dalits, Muslims, and women and girls have safe-violence-free lives with freedom to speak their minds, equal opportunities to realize their rights, and a discrimination-free future. They research to find lasting solutions to end rising inequalities and exclude marginalized communities from getting decent jobs, quality free education and healthcare.
